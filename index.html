CDU IT Code Fair

The CDU IT Code Fair is a great display of Charles Darwin University's talented students.

Come and check out projects by students, learn about industry associations and sign up for events in your high school.

About the 2017 competition

The code fair is an opportunity to share your coding achievements. Enter the competition of your choice and display your hard work! There will be prizes and rewards for outstanding applicants! The competition is open to all students currently enrolled in an IT course at Charles Darwin University. Both individual and group projects are accepted.

Software Competition

The software competition is all about your products (applications, databases, mobile responsive sites and apps). At the event, you will demonstrate working software. What you chose to do is up to you! Find something you're passionate about and apply to enter the competition.

Display your poster to the broader University, IT Industry and interested High Schools by applying to enter.

School Programs

The 2017 Code fair will hold an event for high school students. This code event will engage school students in a fun and interactive learning experience. Students will develop new skills and also get important information on careers within the IT industry.

Hour of Code

This is an introductory crash course in coding. Students will take their very first steps in writing code and have fun along the way. This Hour of Code Workshop has been sponsored by The Discipline of Information Technology, Charles Darwin University in support of the Hour of Code initiative sponsored by Code.org. Register your interest to be involved in this exciting workshop!

Workshop outline

Students will work through a set of activities of varying degrees of difficulty; starting simple and progressing to slightly more challenging tasks. The 2017 workshop will involve:

  • Introduction and overview
  • Interactive activities
  • Introduction to web design
  • Advanced coding topics
  • Resources to take home

CDU Code Fair History

The CDU IT Code Fair is an event that brings the IT community together. This is evident in the success of past events. Every year recognision is given to the achievements of students and feedback from all attendees is documented and taken into consideration for the preparation of future events.
